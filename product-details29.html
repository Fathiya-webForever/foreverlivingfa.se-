Product Description

Forever Bee Pollen™ contains pollen from wildflowers gathered by busy bees, making it a popular and natural dietary supplement. In handy tablet form, this supplement is an easy way to try bee pollen, which has been valued for thousands of years for its various health benefits.

Who is it for?

Forever Bee Pollen™ is perfect for anyone interested in trying a bee product. It is especially popular among those who lead active lifestyles, but it is suitable for most people.

What is Pollen?

Pollen is a floury substance in the pistil of flowers used by plants for reproduction. Bees gather pollen along with nectar, collecting it in pockets on their legs, and bring it back to the hive. It is harvested carefully without harming the bees. Pollen is a vital source of protein for bee communities, used to breed larvae.

Why is it Important for Bees?

Pollen is just as important to bees as nectar, serving as a key protein source for breeding their larvae. Bee communities consume 30–40 kg of pollen per season.

Why Humans Love Bee Pollen?

Humans have valued bee pollen for thousands of years, with Hippocrates among the ancient Greeks using it. Forever Bee Pollen™ offers pollen from a wide range of wildflowers in convenient tablet form, and it also contains honey and royal jelly. The finest raw materials are used, ensuring the greatest care from hive to finished product.

How to Use:

The recommended daily dose is a small dose (1/4 tablet) to help with allergic reactions, especially for people with pollen allergies. The dose can be gradually increased to one tablet three times a day.

Storage Instructions:

Store in a cool, dry place and out of reach of children.
