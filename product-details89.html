Product Description

Aloe Body Wash is the ideal solution for gentle yet effective cleansing. Formulated with skin conditioning Aloe vera, it is pH-balanced and hydrates the skin while cleansing. It leaves your skin feeling fresh, smooth, and rehydrated without any dryness.

Who is it for?

This body wash is suitable for any age and gender. Its luxurious foaming action removes dirt while leaving your skin silky smooth and refreshed. Aloe Body Wash is perfect for anyone seeking a mild yet effective cleanse with added hydration.

Clean and Smooth

The key ingredient, Aloe vera, is known for its conditioning properties. In addition, Aloe Body Wash contains nourishing argan oil, which absorbs quickly and leaves your skin feeling soft. Jojoba esters and arnica flower extract add extra hydration and softness, while vitamin C works as an antioxidant to nourish the skin.

Inspired by the Forest and Sea

The rich foaming formula comes from coconut cleansers, which rinse off easily without stripping moisture. The pleasant fragrance evokes the forest, with hints of amber and fresh herbs, while the deep blue-green color of the wash is inspired by the sea, thanks to the inclusion of copper chlorophyll.

For Energy Metabolism and the Nervous System
