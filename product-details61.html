Product Description

Forever FastBreak™ is a delicious, golden caramel bar with peanut butter crunch and a smooth chocolate-flavored coating. It's an ideal snack for when you're on the go, or a great energy and protein boost before exercise. Perfect for anyone looking for a healthier snack option that still satisfies cravings.

Who is it for?

Forever FastBreak™ is ideal for those looking for a quick, tasty snack or a protein boost before or after exercise. It's suitable for anyone who gets hungry between meals but wants a healthier option that provides essential nutrients.

Key Benefits:

  • Each bar contains 11g of protein to support your muscle needs.
  • Provides 3g of fiber to keep you feeling full longer.
  • Includes essential vitamins and minerals like iron, calcium, vitamin B12, and chromium to support energy metabolism and normal blood glucose levels.
  • Also includes vitamin C to reduce tiredness and fatigue.

Convenient Portion Pack:

Each Forever FastBreak™ bar is individually wrapped, making it easy to carry in your bag for a snack anytime. Stock up for the week or keep a few at your desk to satisfy your cravings or to keep your energy levels up.

Usage:

  • One serving is one 56g bar.

Important Information:

  • This product is an enriched foodstuff and should not be used as a substitute for a varied, balanced diet and a healthy lifestyle.
  • Ensure adequate water intake throughout the day.
  • If you are pregnant, breastfeeding, ill, or on medication, consult a healthcare professional before using enriched products.
  • Store in a cool (under 27°C), dry place and out of reach of children.
