Product Description

The Sonya™ Daily Skincare System is designed for combination skin, offering a gel-based formula enriched with Aloe vera and botanical ingredients. This advanced system deeply hydrates, conditions, and revitalizes your skin, leaving it soft, refreshed, and radiant.

Important Notice

All products in the Sonya™ Daily Skincare System will be discontinued and are only available while stocks last.

Who is it for?

Perfect for all skin types, especially combination skin. If your skin sometimes feels dry, oily, or both, this system is tailored to meet its changing needs.

How It Works

Sonya™ Daily Skincare System combines science and nature with its revolutionary gel-based technology, ensuring that all the Aloe vera, moisture, and botanicals penetrate deep into the skin for maximum effectiveness.

What's Included?

  • Sonya™ Refreshing Gel Cleanser (118 ml) - A gentle foaming cleanser that refreshes and conditions.
  • Sonya™ Illuminating Gel (28.3 g) - Enhances natural glow with encapsulated peptides and botanicals.
  • Sonya™ Refining Gel Mask (59 ml) - Works overnight to balance and hydrate your skin.
  • Sonya™ Soothing Gel Moisturizer (59 ml) - A lightweight gel-lotion that deeply hydrates and softens.

Product Benefits

  • Hydrates and conditions skin with Aloe vera and natural extracts.
  • Improves skin tone, texture, and radiance.
  • Designed specifically for combination skin.
  • Absorbs quickly without feeling greasy.
  • Provides deep moisture for a dewy, refreshed look.

Usage Instructions

  • Refreshing Gel Cleanser: Apply to wet skin, massage gently, and rinse thoroughly.
  • Illuminating Gel: Massage a small amount onto the face for a natural glow.
  • Refining Gel Mask: Apply in the evening, leave overnight, and rinse in the morning. Use 2–3 times a week.
  • Soothing Gel Moisturizer: Massage onto the face and neck to hydrate and condition.

Dermatologically Tested

All four products in the Sonya™ Daily Skincare System were awarded the EXCELLENT rating in dermatological tests performed by Dermatest®.

Tip!

If you need SPF protection, use Protecting Day Lotion SPF 20 in the morning and Sonya™ Soothing Gel Moisturizer in the evening for the perfect skincare combination.

Important Information

For external use only. Avoid direct contact with eyes. If irritation occurs, discontinue use. Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool, dry place.
