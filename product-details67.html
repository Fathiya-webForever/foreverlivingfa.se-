Product Description

The Smoothing Exfoliator helps remove dead skin cells, allowing new, fresh skin to shine through. With a combination of chemical and mechanical exfoliants, it cleanses, conditions, and softens, leaving your skin looking and feeling smoother and more radiant.

Who is it for?

Ideal for anyone looking for a simple yet effective way to refresh their skin. This exfoliator is suitable for all skin types and works by combining gentle exfoliating enzymes with natural cleansing agents.

Key Benefits

  • Removes dead skin cells for a refreshed, glowing complexion.
  • Combines chemical and mechanical exfoliation for effective results.
  • Gently dissolves impurities while maintaining skin hydration.
  • Prepares the skin for better absorption of serums and moisturizers.
  • Softens and conditions skin with Aloe vera and natural fruit extracts.

How It Works

The skin naturally sheds dead cells every day, but this process slows down with age, leading to a dull, dry appearance. The Smoothing Exfoliator speeds up this process with mild exfoliants from papaya, pineapple, and jojoba beads, helping to reveal a smoother and more even skin tone.

Carefully Selected Ingredients

  • Aloe vera - Moisturizes, softens, and conditions the skin.
  • Papain & Bromelain - Enzymes from papaya and pineapple that gently exfoliate.
  • Sodium Lactate - Retains moisture while gently exfoliating.
  • Jojoba Beads & Bamboo Powder - Provide mild mechanical exfoliation for a polished feel.
  • Glycerin - Helps to hydrate and protect the skin barrier.

Usage Instructions

Apply a small amount to your face using moistened fingers. Massage gently in circular motions, avoiding the eye area. Rinse thoroughly with water. Use 2–3 times a week for best results. Follow up with toner, serum, and a moisturizing cream.

Pro Tip!

For morning use, follow up with Protecting Day Lotion SPF 20 to hydrate and protect your skin from harmful UVA and UVB rays.

Dermatologically Tested

The Smoothing Exfoliator was awarded the EXCELLENT rating in dermatological tests conducted by Dermatest®.

Important Information

For external use only. Avoid direct contact with eyes. If irritation occurs, discontinue use. Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool, dry place.
