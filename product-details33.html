Product Description

Forever Aloe Turm™ is a lozenge that combines the powerful benefits of turmeric, Aloe vera, and zinc. Featuring turmeric in a hydrogel form, this lozenge slowly dissolves in your mouth and has a refreshing mint flavor. It’s a delicious, sugar-free way to incorporate turmeric with curcuminoids into your diet, along with the added benefit of zinc.

Who is it for?

This product is perfect for adults who want to include turmeric in their diet in a tasty and convenient form. It also provides zinc, which supports the normal functioning of the immune system. The lozenge is easy to carry, making it a great on-the-go solution.

Key Ingredients:

  • Indian turmeric with curcuminoids
  • Aloe vera
  • Zinc, which supports the immune system and has several other benefits
  • Hydrogel technology for optimal absorption

Indian Turmeric

Turmeric, derived from the root of a plant in the ginger family, has been used for centuries for its many benefits. Forever Aloe Turm uses the highest quality turmeric sourced from India, bringing you a powerful and natural addition to your wellness routine.

What Does Zinc Do?

Zinc is an essential mineral that plays a vital role in many body functions. It helps maintain a healthy immune system, protects cells from oxidative stress, and supports cognitive function. Zinc also promotes healthy bones, hair, nails, and skin, and is important for normal vision.

Always Ready

Forever Aloe Turm comes in a handy blister pack of 10 lozenges, making it easy to carry around in your bag. Whenever you need it, simply place a lozenge under your tongue or in your cheek and let it slowly dissolve. It's a perfect way to freshen your breath while providing valuable nutrients!

How to Use:

Take up to 3 lozenges a day. Allow each lozenge to slowly dissolve in your mouth (not to be swallowed whole). Store the blister pack in the box. Not suitable for people on blood-thinning medication. Keep in a cool, dry place out of reach of children.

Important Information:

  • Not suitable for individuals on blood-thinning medication.
  • Do not exceed the recommended daily dose.
  • Dietary supplements should not be used as a substitute for a varied diet.
  • Store in a cool, dry place.
