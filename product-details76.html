Product Description

Forever Aloe Scrub™ is a gentle yet effective exfoliant that helps cleanse and refresh your skin without drying it out. Made with Aloe vera and natural jojoba oil, it exfoliates while nourishing the skin.

Who is it for?

This exfoliator is suitable for everyone, from young to old, and for all skin types. It’s perfect for anyone who wants to give their skin a deep cleanse and rejuvenate its appearance.

Why Exfoliate?

Exfoliation removes dead skin cells and helps maintain healthy, radiant skin. As we age, skin renewal slows down, leading to dull and dry skin. Regular exfoliation not only helps improve skin appearance but also allows skincare products to penetrate more effectively.

Gentle Yet Effective

  • Aloe Vera – Softens, moisturizes, and conditions the skin.
  • Jojoba Oil Beads – Gently exfoliate and remove impurities.
  • Sodium Lactate – Helps with exfoliation and moisture retention.
  • Coconut-derived Cleansers – Cleanse without drying the skin.
  • Vitamins C & E – Skin conditioning and antioxidant properties.

Usage Instructions

Apply to damp skin and gently massage in a rotating motion for 2-3 minutes, avoiding the eyes. Use additional water as necessary. Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water and pat dry with a soft towel. Finish with your favorite moisturizer for best results.

Dermatologically Tested

Forever Aloe Scrub™ has been dermatologically tested and awarded the rating EXCELLENT by Dermatest®.

Important Information

For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. If irritation occurs, discontinue use. Store in a cool, dry place.
