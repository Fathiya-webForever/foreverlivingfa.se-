Product Description

Replenishing Skin Oil is an exclusive formula designed to enhance your skin’s elasticity and smoothness. This luxurious oil conditions and soothes the skin while making it soft and supple. It’s suitable for sensitive skin and free from silicon, providing a premium skincare experience.

Who is it for?

This product is perfect for anyone looking to nourish and care for their skin, particularly those with mature skin. It is suitable for all skin types and ages, and ideal for anyone seeking a high-quality product to keep their skin soft and youthful.

Key Benefits

  • Conditions and soothes the skin with borage seed oil, Aloe vera, and bisabolol.
  • Rich in vitamins A, C, and E to help maintain youthful skin.
  • Suitable for all skin types, even sensitive skin.
  • Helps to retain moisture and gives the skin a soft, smooth, and supple feel.

Skin-Conditioning Ingredients

Replenishing Skin Oil contains a blend of nourishing botanical ingredients and vitamins to promote a healthy, glowing complexion:

  • Borage Seed Oil: Softens and conditions the skin, with a soothing effect on dry and irritated skin.
  • Vitamin A (Retinyl Palmitate): Known for its smoothing effect, helping reduce fine lines and wrinkles.
  • Vitamin C and E: Antioxidants that protect skin cells from oxidative stress and contribute to a protective barrier on the skin.
  • Aloe Vera: Moisturizes and softens the skin.
  • Bisabolol: A soothing ingredient from the Eremanthus plant that helps calm and soften the skin.

Usage Instructions

After cleansing your face, apply a couple of drops of Replenishing Skin Oil to your face and neck. Gently massage in with your fingertips before applying your face lotion.

Ingredients List

Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride, C15-19 Alkane, Tocopheryl Acetate, Hydrogenated Polyisobutene, Retinyl Palmitate, Borago Officinalis Seed Oil, Parfum, Bisabolol, Lecithin, Carthamus Tinctorius Seed Oil, Glycine Soja Oil, Limonene, Linalool, Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Extract, Geraniol, Citronellol, Tocopherol, Ascorbyl Palmitate.

Important Information

For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. If irritation occurs, discontinue use. Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool, dry place.
