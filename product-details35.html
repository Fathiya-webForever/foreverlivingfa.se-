Product Description

Forever Absorbent-D is a chewable tablet that delivers vitamin D in a delicious, fruity form. Infused with vitamin E and prickly pear for a sweet, fresh flavor, it provides an enjoyable way to meet your daily vitamin D needs.

Who is it for?

This product is for all adults who want to ensure they’re getting their daily dose of vitamin D, with the added bonus of a tasty prickly pear flavor.

Key Ingredients:

  • Vitamin D (from sheep’s wool)
  • Vitamin E (D-alpha-tocopheryl acetate)
  • Prickly pear juice powder (11%)
  • Fructose and sweeteners

Water- and Fat-Soluble Vitamins

Vitamins are classified into water-soluble or fat-soluble based on how they are processed and stored by your body. Vitamin D is a fat-soluble vitamin, meaning it is stored in your body and released as needed. It plays an essential role in the maintenance of muscle function, immune system function, and the absorption of calcium and phosphorus.

Benefits of Vitamin D

Vitamin D3 is the form of vitamin D produced when your skin is exposed to sunlight. It can also be obtained from certain foods such as fish, meat, and eggs. Forever Absorbent-D uses vitamin D3 sourced from sheep’s wool.

Antioxidants

Along with vitamin D, Forever Absorbent-D contains vitamin E, a powerful antioxidant that helps protect cells from oxidative stress. Like vitamin D, vitamin E is also fat-soluble.

Sweet Prickly Pear Flavor

Forever Absorbent-D features a bright pink color and a fruity taste derived from prickly pear, a fruit that blends the flavors of melon, raspberry, and strawberry.

How to Use:

The recommended daily dose is two chewable tablets—one tablet, twice a day. Store the product in a cool, dry place, and keep tightly closed. Do not use if the seal is broken or missing.

Important Information:

  • Do not exceed the recommended daily dose.
  • Dietary supplements should not be used as a substitute for a varied and balanced diet and a healthy lifestyle.
  • Keep out of reach of children.
  • Excessive consumption may cause laxative effects.
