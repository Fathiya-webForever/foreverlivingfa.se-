Who is it for?

This supplement is perfect for anyone curious to try the queen bee's special food and looking for a nutrient-dense addition to their diet.

What is Royal Jelly?

Royal jelly is a milky-white secretion produced by bees to nourish the queen bee. While pollen is gathered from plants, royal jelly is created by the bees themselves as part of their digestion process. It is fed to all newborn bees for the first few days of their lives, and afterward, it is reserved for those destined to become queens.

A Bee Product from an Untouched Environment

Forever Royal Jelly™ is sourced from bees in dry, remote areas at high altitudes, which provide the perfect natural environment for beekeeping. The royal jelly is carefully harvested using specialized equipment and is then freeze-dried to maintain its nutrients. You can trust that Forever's bee-derived products have been made with the utmost care and quality.
