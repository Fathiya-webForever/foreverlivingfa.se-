Product Description

The Aloe Bio-Cellulose Mask is an innovative, easy-to-use face mask that delivers intense hydration and anti-aging benefits to your skin. In just 20 minutes, your skin will feel refreshed, moisturized, and glowing. Made from bio-cellulose, a material known for retaining moisture, this mask ensures your skin gets the maximum benefit from the ingredients.

Who is it for?

This face mask is suitable for all skin types and is perfect for those looking to revitalize dull and tired skin. It’s especially great for those who want a quick, effective skincare routine without the need for rinsing afterward. It is fragrance-free, vegan-friendly, and compostable, making it an excellent eco-conscious skincare option.

Key Benefits

  • Intensively rehydrates the skin with anti-aging properties.
  • Helps improve skin tone and adds a youthful glow.
  • Contains skin-conditioning ingredients such as Aloe vera, jojoba seed oil, and sodium hyaluronate.
  • Easy to use with no rinsing required—simply massage the serum into your skin.
  • Suitable for all skin types and free from fragrance, making it ideal for sensitive skin.

Ingredients

The mask is enriched with a serum containing the finest Aloe vera, along with other hydrating and soothing ingredients like glycerin, sodium hyaluronate, olive oil fatty acids, and jojoba seed oil. These ingredients work together to deeply moisturize and rejuvenate your skin.

Usage Instructions

  1. Open the bag and unfold the mask.
  2. Remove the protective woven fabric on both sides of the mask.
  3. Place the mask on your face and gently press it to ensure a firm fit.
  4. Remove the second layer of fabric.
  5. Leave the mask on for about 20 minutes, then remove it and discard. No need to rinse—just massage any remaining serum into your face and neck for a youthful glow.

Important Information

For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. If irritation occurs, discontinue use. Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool, dry place.
