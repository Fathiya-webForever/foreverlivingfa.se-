A Fine Male Fragrance

Forever Malosi Fine Fragrance is an enticing blend of warm, spicy notes like bergamot and sandalwood, perfectly balanced with refreshing citrus and cypress aromas. Inspired by Samoa’s tropical beaches and clear waters, this fragrance embodies power and hospitality.

For Best Results

Fragrances degrade when exposed to heat, light, or air. Store your fragrance in a cool, dry place—such as the fridge—for longevity. Applying a fragrance-free lotion beforehand helps the scent last longer.

Where Should I Apply the Fragrance?
