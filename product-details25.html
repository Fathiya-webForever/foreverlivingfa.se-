Product Description

FAB Forever Active Boost™ is the perfect energy drink to energize your day with guarana, taurine, and caffeine. Packed with B vitamins and a vibrant tutti frutti flavor, it quickly gets you back on track when you need it the most. With added cranberry and acerola extracts, this drink will refresh and hydrate you while giving you a powerful energy boost to keep you alert and focused.

Who is it for?

This drink is designed for adults who want an extra energy boost. It contains guarana, taurine, and caffeine, as well as essential B vitamins, making it ideal for those needing a pick-me-up during the day, especially at the office or during workouts.

Key Ingredients:

  • Guarana seed extract
  • Taurine
  • Caffeine
  • Acerola fruit powder
  • Cranberry juice extract
  • B vitamins (B2, B3, B6, B12)

How to Use:

Shake gently before use. Serve chilled for maximum refreshment and energy. Recommended daily intake: one can per day. This product is not suitable for children, pregnant, or breastfeeding women.

Additional Benefits:

FAB Forever Active Boost helps support normal energy-yielding metabolism and reduce tiredness and fatigue. It also promotes mental focus and alertness thanks to the combination of guarana, taurine, and B vitamins. Plus, it has a refreshing tutti frutti flavor with the added benefits of cranberry and acerola fruit powder.

Storage Instructions:

Store in a cool, dry place. Once opened, refrigerate and consume within a reasonable time frame for best quality.
