Product Description

Everyday luxury. Aloe Liquid Soap combines skin-conditioning Aloe vera and argan oil with natural cleansers from coconut, for example. Treat yourself to a luxuriously clean, fresh feeling, without drying your skin out.

Who is it for?

Aloe Liquid Soap is great for the whole family, including those with sensitive skin. It can be used over the whole body and has a lovely, fresh – but not too strong – scent.

Key Ingredients:

  • Aloe vera
  • Argan oil
  • Coconut-based cleansers
  • Jojoba esters
  • Cucumber extract
  • Arnica flower extract

How to Use:

Add a small amount of Aloe Liquid Soap to wet hands. Gently work into a lather and wash thoroughly. Aloe Liquid Soap can be used over the whole body. Rinse thoroughly after use.

Additional Benefits:

Aloe Liquid Soap is mild enough to be used daily, even on sensitive skin. It produces a silky lather and leaves your skin feeling luxuriously clean and fresh. Comes in a practical, hygienic pump bottle – perfect for the washbasin or shower.

Quality Assurance:
