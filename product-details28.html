Product Description

Forever Plant Protein is a quick, simple, and delicious vegan protein shake that provides 17 grams of protein per portion. It's perfect for those with an active lifestyle who need a nutritious and easy snack. Simply blend it with water or unsweetened plant-based milk and enjoy a creamy vanilla-flavored shake that supports muscle maintenance and recovery.

Who is it for?

This product is ideal for anyone looking for a plant-based protein boost. Whether you need a post-workout recovery shake, a quick snack on the go, or simply want to support your health with a vegan formula, Forever Plant Protein is the solution. It’s also perfect for people who want a dairy-free, soya-free protein option.

Key Benefits:

  • 17 grams of protein per serving
  • Fully vegan formula
  • Contains pea and pumpkin protein
  • Dairy and soya free
  • Creamy vanilla flavor

How to Use:

For the recommended daily dose, mix 2 scoops (52 g) of Forever Plant Protein with 240 ml of water or unsweetened plant-based milk (e.g., soy, oat, almond, cashew, or coconut milk). Shake well before use. This product is ideal for a protein-rich recovery shake after training or as a convenient snack on the go.

Ingredients:

  • Pea protein isolate (59%)
  • Pumpkin protein powder (26%)
  • Vanilla flavor
  • Maltodextrin
  • Fructooligosaccharides
  • Medium chain triglycerides
  • Guar gum (thickener)
  • Aloe vera juice powder
  • Sweetener (steviol glycosides)

Storage Instructions:

Store in a cool, dry place out of reach of children.

Nutritional Values (Per Recommended Daily Dose - 2 Scoops / 52 g):

  • Pea protein: 26 g (52% RI)
  • Pumpkin protein: 8 g (16% RI)
  • Aloe vera juice powder: 36 mg
