Product Description

Forever Ultra™ Chocolate is a tasty and calorie-conscious meal replacement shake. With high-value soy protein, essential vitamins, and minerals, this shake offers maximum nutrition with minimum calories. It’s perfect for weight control, a post-exercise protein boost, or simply as a snack when you’re craving something delicious.

Who is it for?

Ideal for adults looking to maintain a healthy lifestyle. Forever Ultra™ is a perfect meal replacement for weight control or as a high-protein snack after exercise. It’s designed to be mixed with milk to provide you with all the necessary nutrition to replace one or two meals per day.

Key Benefits:

  • Contains 21g of high-quality soy protein per serving to maintain muscle mass.
  • Helps control your calorie intake with only around 200 kcal per serving.
  • Provides essential vitamins and minerals, including Vitamin C, B vitamins, calcium, magnesium, and iron.
  • Supports normal muscle function and energy metabolism.

How it Works:

Forever Ultra™ is not only a great meal replacement, but also a source of high-quality protein that supports muscle maintenance. By mixing it with milk, you get a complete meal with fewer calories, perfect for managing your weight. It's also a great option to keep you full while still providing the nutrients you need.

Usage:

  • Mix 1 level measuring spoon (equivalent to 25g) with 240 ml of semi-skimmed milk (1.5% fat).
  • Shake well before use and consume immediately.

Important Information:

  • Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry, dark place.
  • For adults. This meal replacement should be used as part of a healthy diet and lifestyle.
  • It is important to drink plenty of fluids throughout the day.
  • Do not use if the packaging is torn or damaged.
