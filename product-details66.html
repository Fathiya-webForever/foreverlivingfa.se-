Product Description

The Protecting Day Lotion is a lightweight, moisturizing face lotion with SPF 20 that shields your skin from harmful UVA and UVB rays. Infused with botanical ingredients and Aloe vera, it conditions, hydrates, and helps maintain youthful, radiant skin.

Who is it for?

This lotion is for anyone looking for daily sun protection while keeping their skin soft, supple, and well-moisturized. A great choice for year-round protection, whether in summer or winter.

Key Benefits

  • SPF 20 for protection against UVA and UVB rays.
  • Lightweight formula that absorbs quickly without greasiness.
  • Contains Aloe vera for hydration and skin conditioning.
  • Rich in botanical oils like sunflower seed oil, rapeseed oil, and castor oil for extra nourishment.
  • Includes vitamin E and vitamin C for antioxidant protection against free radicals.
  • Infused with elemi, a botanical extract known for its skin-conditioning properties.

How It Works

The combination of zinc oxide, a natural sun filter, and antioxidant-rich ingredients protects the skin from harmful UV rays and environmental damage. The lotion deeply hydrates without feeling heavy, making it perfect for daily use.

Usage Instructions

Apply evenly to the face and neck every morning after cleansing. Use daily for optimal sun protection and hydration.

Why Sun Protection Matters

UVA rays can contribute to premature aging, wrinkles, and pigmentation. UVB rays primarily cause sunburn. Sun protection is essential year-round, as UVA rays can penetrate glass and cause damage even when you're indoors.

Important Information

For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. If irritation occurs, discontinue use. Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool, dry place.
