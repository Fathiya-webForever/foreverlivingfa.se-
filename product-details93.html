Product Description

A plant-based conditioning hair oil with Aloe vera and six botanical oils. Rehydrates, adds shine, and fights frizz and split ends. Completely silicon-free.

What You Get

  • Rehydration, shine, and frizz control
  • Six nourishing botanical oils
  • Silicon-free formula
  • Precise dosing with included pipette

Botanical Oils for Nourished Hair

This lightweight, silicon-free formula is powered by six botanical oils:

  • Abyssinian oil: Strengthens and protects hair from styling and environmental stress.
  • Jojoba oil: Adds shine and makes hair easier to brush.
  • Pataua oil: Rich in antioxidants to smooth and soothe hair.
  • Tsubaki oil: Extracted from the Japanese camellia plant, improves elasticity.
  • Pequi oil: A Brazilian treasure that adds shine and reduces frizz.
  • Safflower oil: Deeply hydrates and softens for healthy hair.

Silky-Smooth Anti-Frizz Formula

Forever’s Nourishing Hair Oil contains plant-based silicon alternatives that strengthen hair and protect it from damage. Beetroot extract gives your hair a silky-smooth feel and healthy shine. The lightweight formula absorbs quickly without weighing hair down or making it greasy. Works perfectly with Aloe-Jojoba Shampoo & Conditioner.

Who is it for?

Suitable for all hair types, this nourishing oil tames frizz and split ends while leaving hair healthy, shiny, and silky-smooth.

Dermatologically Tested

Nourishing Hair Oil was awarded the seal EXCELLENT in dermatological testing conducted by Dermatest®.

How to Use

Apply a few drops to your palm, rub hands together, and distribute evenly through damp hair, from the ends up to mid-length. Can also be used on dry hair after styling.
