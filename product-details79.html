Product Description

Firming Serum is packed with active ingredients that add extra moisture to the skin, reducing the appearance of fine lines. It's a highly effective anti-ageing supplement for your skincare routine, providing that extra boost of hydration and conditioning.

Who is it for?

Everyone who wants to keep their skin fresh, supple, and youthful will benefit from this serum. Whether your skin is young or mature, Firming Serum offers that extra care to keep your skin looking its best for longer.

Fine Ingredients Working Together

The key ingredient in Firming Serum is Aloe Vera, renowned for its skin-conditioning, moisturizing, and softening properties. The serum enhances Aloe Vera's effectiveness with a range of active ingredients, including:

  • Tripeptide-1, Trifluoroacetyl Tripeptide-2, and Caprooyl Tetrapeptide-3 – These peptides mimic the skin's natural processes to give the skin a firmer, more elastic feel.
  • Yeast Extract – Helps even out skin tone and improve overall skin texture.
  • Whey Protein – Improves skin firmness and softness.
  • Glycerine – Forms a moisture-binding barrier on the skin to help retain hydration.
  • Sodium Hyaluronate – A salt form of hyaluronic acid that helps the skin retain moisture.
  • Lactic Acid and Lactose – Enhance moisture retention for a fresh, hydrated feel.
  • L-Arginine and Vitamin C – Skin conditioning agents that help improve the overall health of the skin.

Combating the Signs of Ageing

While we can’t stop time, we can slow down the visible signs of ageing. Firming Serum is a natural anti-ageing solution for your skincare routine, providing deep hydration and improving skin elasticity. This lightweight formula is quickly absorbed without leaving any sticky residue, making it an ideal choice for daily use.

Dermatologically Tested

infinite by Forever™ Firming Serum has earned an EXCELLENT rating in dermatological tests performed by Dermatest®.

Usage Instructions

Apply 1–2 doses of serum to cleansed skin. Gently massage the serum into your face and neck until absorbed. For optimal results, apply a further pump of serum onto the neck, sweeping upwards towards the chin. The serum is best used after cleansing, but before applying a moisturizing face lotion. Use daily, both morning and evening.
