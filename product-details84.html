Product Description

Give your skin a natural glow with Sonya™ Illuminating Gel. This product is designed to restore your skin’s natural radiance and improve its overall appearance. Thanks to encapsulated peptides and powerful botanicals, your skin will enjoy a healthy, radiant glow with regular use.

Who is it for?

Sonya™ Illuminating Gel is suitable for anyone who wants to enhance their skin's natural glow. It’s great for all ages and skin types, especially combination skin, which can sometimes feel dry or greasy. If you feel your skin lacks that youthful radiance, this product is for you!

Just Another Day for You – A Challenge for Your Skin

Our skin faces challenges daily – from stress and air pollution to sun exposure, wind, and cold. Over time, these factors can dull our skin’s natural glow. Sonya™ Illuminating Gel helps restore that lost radiance, brightening and evening out your complexion.

Restore Your Skin’s Natural Radiance

The star ingredient in this gel is Aloe vera, known for its moisturizing, softening, and conditioning properties. To amplify these effects, the gel also contains brightening peptides and potent botanicals that work together to bring your skin a healthy glow. The gel absorbs easily and doesn’t leave any greasy residue, giving you soft, smooth, even-toned skin.

With Powerful Ingredients

Sonya™ Illuminating Gel is packed with natural, skin-conditioning ingredients like jujuberry, capillary wormwood, and haptophyte algae (Dicrateria rotunda and Ruttnera lamellosa). Liquorice root adds a brightening effect while Baikal skullcap helps retain moisture and firms the skin. White mulberry further conditions and brightens the skin, leaving it with a smoother appearance and an unbeatable natural glow.

Dermatologically Tested

Sonya™ Illuminating Gel received the rating EXCELLENT in dermatological tests performed by Dermatest®.

More Products in the Series

Sonya™ Daily Skincare System also includes: Sonya™ Refreshing Gel Cleanser, Sonya™ Refining Gel Mask, and Sonya™ Soothing Gel Moisturizer. These products work together to provide a complete and balanced skincare routine.

Usage Instructions

Carefully massage a small amount of Sonya™ Illuminating Gel over your entire face for a radiant glow. For best results, incorporate this product into your skincare routine alongside Sonya™ Refreshing Gel Cleanser, Sonya™ Soothing Gel Moisturizer, and Sonya™ Refining Gel Mask.
