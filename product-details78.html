Product Description

Hydrating Cleanser is a light, mild, moisture-retaining cleanser that removes dirt, oil, and makeup, all while taking care of your sensitive facial skin. It effectively cleanses without drying, leaving your skin feeling fresh and hydrated.

Who is it for?

This cleanser is perfect for all skin types, especially those with sensitive skin. It’s a great foundation product for your skincare routine, helping cleanse your skin morning and evening to keep it fresh and clean. It’s essential for removing makeup, pollution, and other impurities accumulated throughout the day.

The First Step in Your Skincare Routine

Cleansing morning and night is crucial to maintain fresh, glowing skin. Evening cleansing is especially important to remove makeup, pollution, and sweat residues. A properly cleansed face also ensures that your skincare products, such as day or night creams, work effectively.

Cleanses Without Irritating

The Hydrating Cleanser contains Aloe Vera, which is known for its moisturizing, conditioning, and soothing properties. It also includes sunflower oil, rich in vitamin E, which gently dissolves dirt and removes impurities without irritating the skin. Additionally, coconut, apple amino acid, and glycerine-based ingredients work together to nourish the skin while keeping it hydrated and soft.

Protects and Conditions Your Skin

  • Capryloyl Glycerine – Protects and conditions the skin, leaving it silky smooth.
  • Glycerine – Forms a protective barrier that helps retain moisture in the skin.
  • L-Arginine – A conditioning amino acid that contributes to skin hydration.
  • Vitamins C & E – Known for their antioxidant properties and skin conditioning effects.

No Sulphates or Fragrance

Our Hydrating Cleanser is free from perfume and sulphates, making it gentle enough for sensitive skin while still offering great cleansing performance.

Dermatologically Tested

infinite by Forever™ Hydrating Cleanser has received an EXCELLENT rating in dermatological tests performed by Dermatest®.

Usage Instructions

Apply a generous amount to wet fingers and work the cleanser gently in a circular motion around the face and neck. Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water. For best results, follow up with a toner and a moisturizing face lotion or serum. Use daily, both morning and evening.
