Product Description

For real beauty sleep, Sonya™ Refining Gel Mask works overnight to support your skin’s natural balance, controlling oiliness and delivering moisture. Wake up to brighter, fresher-looking skin with this delightful cooling gel that works while you sleep.

Who is it for?

This product is ideal for those who prefer a simplified skincare routine. It is suitable for all ages and skin types, especially combination skin, which can feel dry at times, greasy at others, or somewhere in between. Sonya™ Refining Gel Mask makes it easy to achieve balanced, glowing skin with minimal effort.

Beauty Sleep Worthy of the Name

It’s well-known that good sleep helps keep your skin fresh. Sonya™ Refining Gel Mask maximizes your beauty sleep with a cooling gel packed with botanicals like carrot seed oil, soja oil, basil oil, and davana oil. The latter has a calming, relaxing effect and is ideal for restful, rejuvenating sleep.

Aloe Vera in the Starring Role

The gel is absorbed into your skin, delivering the effects of Aloe vera, a key ingredient known for its skin-conditioning, moisturizing, and softening properties. The mask also contains apple, red clover, and Sasa quelpaertensis (Korean bamboo) extract, all of which condition and firm the skin. Liquorice root adds smoothing, soothing, and brightening benefits, helping your skin wake up with a radiant, healthy glow.

As Simple as It Gets

Instead of your regular moisturizer, use Sonya™ Refining Gel Mask 2–3 times a week before bed. The result? Radiant, balanced skin that feels more youthful by the morning.

Dermatologically Tested

Sonya™ Refining Gel Mask received the rating EXCELLENT in dermatological tests performed by Dermatest®.

More Products in the Series

Sonya™ Daily Skincare System also includes: Sonya™ Refreshing Gel Cleanser, Sonya™ Illuminating Gel, and Sonya™ Soothing Gel Moisturizer. These products work together for a complete, easy skincare routine.

Usage Instructions

Apply a generous amount of Sonya™ Refining Gel Mask over your entire face in the evening. Leave it on overnight and rinse thoroughly in the morning for balanced, radiant skin. Use 2–3 times a week instead of Sonya™ Soothing Gel Moisturizer for best results.
