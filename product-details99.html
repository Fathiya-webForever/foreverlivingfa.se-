Product Description

Boost your daily Aloe vera shot with the power of ARGI+! This convenient set combines Aloe Vera Gel with L-Arginine and essential vitamins to support overall wellness.

Key Benefits

  • Enhances your daily Aloe vera intake with ARGI+
  • Provides Vitamin C, B6, B12, folic acid, and Vitamin D
  • Contains L-Arginine, an important amino acid
  • Supports energy, circulation, and immune function
  • Convenient 9-day supply

What’s inside the set?

  • 3 x Forever Aloe Vera Gel™ (330 ml)
  • 3 x ARGI+™ portion packs (10 g)
  • 3 x ARGI+™ informational folders

How to use

Mix one portion pack of ARGI+™ into a 330 ml Forever Aloe Vera Gel bottle. Store in the refrigerator and shake before serving. Each set provides a 9-day wellness boost!

Usage Instructions

Forever Aloe Vera Gel™

Recommended daily dose: 3 portions of 40 ml mixed with 240 ml of water or juice.

Storage: Once opened, store in the fridge (max 7°C). Consume within 30 days.

ARGI+™

Recommended daily dose: 1 portion pack (10 g) mixed with 240 ml of water or beverage.

This product is a dietary supplement for adults. Do not exceed the recommended daily dose. Store in a cool, dry place and keep out of reach of children.

Ingredients

Forever Aloe Vera Gel™

Aloe vera inner gel (99.7%), Vitamin C, antioxidant, acidity regulator (citric acid).

ARGI+™

Contains L-arginine, Vitamin C, D-ribose, fruit juice powders (black currant, blackberry, elderberry, raspberry, cherry, blueberry), grape and pomegranate extracts, Vitamin D3, Vitamin K2, Vitamin B6, Vitamin B12, and folic acid.

Allergen Information

Contains sulphites. Includes sugar and sweeteners.
