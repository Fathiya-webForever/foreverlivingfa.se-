Product Description

The perfect cream for slightly coarser skin. R3 Factor™ gently removes dead skin cells while restoring the skin’s moisture balance and elasticity. Enriched with Aloe vera, AHAs, vitamins A, C, and E, and collagen.

Who is it for?

Ideal for both men and women with slightly coarser skin who want to restore softness and suppleness. Also perfect as a temporary treatment for dry, dull, or lifeless skin.

Key Benefits

  • Removes dead skin cells to reveal fresh, healthy skin.
  • Restores moisture balance and elasticity.
  • Contains Aloe vera, fruit acids (AHAs), vitamins, and collagen.
  • Supports skin renewal for a radiant complexion.
  • Softens, conditions, and protects the skin.

How It Works

Alpha Hydroxy Acids (AHAs), also known as fruit acids, help dissolve the substances that bind dead skin cells, allowing new, healthy cells to emerge. Regular use ensures smoother, softer, and more radiant skin.

Powerful Ingredients

  • Aloe Vera – Hydrates, soothes, and conditions the skin.
  • AHAs (Fruit Acids) – Promote gentle exfoliation for skin renewal.
  • Vitamins A, C & E – Nourish and protect the skin from environmental stress.
  • White Tea Extract – Helps restore and protect the skin.
  • Squalane – Softens and smooths the skin.
  • Sugar Cane & Collagen – Moisturize and condition the skin.

Usage Instructions

Apply to the face and neck after cleansing, morning and evening. For best results, follow up with the products from one of our skincare series during the day.

Dermatologically Tested

R3 Factor™ has been dermatologically tested and awarded the rating EXCELLENT by Dermatest®.

Important Information

For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. If irritation occurs, discontinue use. Store in a cool, dry place.
