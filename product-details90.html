Product Description

Forever Aloe Lips™ is the ultimate lip balm with Aloe vera, jojoba oil, and beeswax, ensuring that your lips stay soft, smooth, and well-protected against harsh weather conditions. Ideal for both urban fashionistas and outdoor adventurers, it’s a must-have for healthy lips every day, wherever you are.

Who is it for?

Forever Aloe Lips™ is suitable for anyone seeking soft, smooth lips, regardless of their lifestyle. Whether you're braving the outdoors or simply seeking comfort for your lips, this balm is perfect for everyone.

What You Get

  • An effective lip balm with smart packaging
  • Skin-conditioning Aloe vera and softening jojoba oil
  • Protective beeswax and soothing allantoin
  • No added water – perfect for cold and windy conditions

Why Do We Get Dry Lips?

Dry, flaky lips are common when exposed to cold, wind, or sun. Licking your lips can make things worse by removing natural moisture. This is where Forever Aloe Lips™ steps in to provide the relief your lips need.

Conditions, Softens, Protects, and Soothes

With Aloe vera, jojoba oil, and beeswax, Forever Aloe Lips™ helps to keep your lips soft while protecting them from the elements. Aloe vera and jojoba oil provide emollient properties, while beeswax shields your lips from harsh conditions. Allantoin adds a soothing touch, making it versatile enough to use on cuticles and insect bites too.

Dermatologically Tested

Forever Aloe Lips™ has received an EXCELLENT rating in dermatological tests conducted by Dermatest®.

Usage Instructions

Use regularly as a lip balm, especially during extreme weather conditions. It can also be applied to insect bites and cuticles for added relief.
