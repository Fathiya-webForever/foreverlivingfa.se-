Product Description

Awakening Eye Cream is designed to reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles around the eyes by delivering intense moisture and nourishment. The delicate skin around your eyes shows the first signs of stress, lack of sleep, and aging, making this eye cream a powerful remedy to restore its youthful look.

Who is it for?

This product is perfect for anyone—both men and women—who wish to reduce fine lines and wrinkles. It’s an ideal choice for those who need extra hydration to smooth the skin around their eyes.

Traditional Meets Modern

The Awakening Eye Cream combines traditional botanicals and modern skincare. Aloe vera, known for its skin-conditioning properties, is the main ingredient. Additionally, jojoba softens and soothes, while beetroot and silk tree bark extract help condition the skin. Four different peptide chains offer humectant, skin conditioning, and softening benefits.

The Fight Against Fine Lines and Wrinkles

As we age, collagen production decreases, causing skin to lose firmness and leading to the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. Awakening Eye Cream contains hydrolyzed collagen, broken down into smaller components for easier absorption. Additionally, xylitol and sodium hyaluronate (hyaluronic acid salt) hydrate and condition the skin, while Dunaliella algae extract offers further conditioning benefits.

A Vital Step in Your Skincare Routine

This eye cream, free from silicones and perfume, is suitable for all skin types. Its silky-smooth texture absorbs quickly, leaving the skin nourished and hydrated. It’s an essential product for maintaining the delicate skin around your eyes and keeping it looking youthful.

Dermatologically Tested

Awakening Eye Cream was awarded the rating EXCELLENT in dermatological tests performed by Dermatest®.

More Products in the Series

Our advanced skincare series also includes: smoothing exfoliator, aloe bio-cellulose mask, protecting day lotion SPF 20, aloe activator, and balancing toner. It pairs beautifully with the infinite by Forever™ series and Sonya™ daily skincare system.

Usage Instructions

Place a small amount of cream onto your fingertip and gently dab around the eyes. Use daily after cleansing for best results.
