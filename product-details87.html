Product Description

Aloe Activator offers mild skincare with a variety of applications. It can be used on its own as a soothing, conditioning moisturizer or combined with Mask Powder to create a rejuvenating aloe mask. It's especially beneficial for those with sensitive skin and can also be used to soothe and cool under-eye bags.

Who is it for?

Aloe Activator is perfect for anyone seeking gentle skin care. It's particularly ideal for sensitive skin and can be used alone as a moisturizer or with Mask Powder to create a luxurious aloe mask. Its calming properties make it perfect for relieving skin irritation and cooling tired eyes.

Few Yet Effective Ingredients

The key ingredient in Aloe Activator is Aloe vera, known for its skin-conditioning, emollient, and moisturizing benefits. It also contains allantoin, a gentle and soothing compound often found in products for sensitive skin. Additionally, the formula is enriched with Vitamin C, an antioxidant known for its skin-conditioning properties.

Use with Mask Powder or on Its Own

Aloe Activator can be used in various ways. It makes an excellent face lotion on its own or can be mixed with Mask Powder (Item No. 341) to create an aloe mask. To make the mask, simply mix 1 tsp of Mask Powder with 1 tsp of Aloe Activator and apply to cleansed skin. After 30 minutes, rinse off with warm water. For best results, use 2–3 times a week.

Usage Instructions

Apply Aloe Activator to a cotton pad and gently wipe it over the face to cleanse and freshen the skin. For an aloe mask, mix 1 tsp of Mask Powder with 1 tsp of Aloe Activator, apply to cleansed skin, and leave on for 30 minutes. Rinse thoroughly with warm water. Use 2–3 times a week for fresh, hydrated skin.
