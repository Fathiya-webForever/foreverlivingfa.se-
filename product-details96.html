Product Description

A powerful yet gentle conditioner that leaves hair soft, shiny, and easy to style. Pairs perfectly with Aloe-Jojoba Shampoo and is free from silicones.

What You Get

  • Soft, lustrous, and fuller hair
  • Jojoba, aloe vera, and argan oil for nourishment
  • Silicone-free formula for a natural, healthy finish
  • Lightweight, non-greasy conditioning

Who is it for?

Aloe-Jojoba Conditioner is suitable for all hair types. Its lightweight formula conditions without weighing hair down, making it ideal for those who want softness and volume. It has the same tropical fragrance with star fruit and melon notes as Aloe-Jojoba Shampoo, making it popular with both men and women.

Healthy Hair Starts at the Scalp

Many hair problems start with a dry or irritated scalp. Aloe vera and jojoba oil work together to hydrate the scalp, while argan and rosehip oils strengthen the hair and restore moisture, ensuring soft and nourished locks.

Nourishing Ingredients

Aloe vera and jojoba oil condition and hydrate both hair and scalp, while argan oil strengthens and softens. Rosehip oil provides essential nutrients, and vitamin C helps maintain a balanced pH level, supporting healthy and hydrated hair.

The Perfect Finish

Light yet powerful, Aloe-Jojoba Conditioner delivers soft, silky, and voluminous hair without weighing it down. It’s mild enough for daily use and suitable for the whole family.

Dermatologically Tested

Aloe-Jojoba Conditioner was awarded the rating EXCELLENT in dermatological tests conducted by Dermatest®.

How to Use

After shampooing with Aloe-Jojoba Shampoo, massage Aloe-Jojoba Conditioner into the hair and leave it in for 2–3 minutes. Rinse thoroughly and let hair dry naturally or style as desired.
