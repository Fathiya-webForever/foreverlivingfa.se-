Product Description

Forever Focus™ is a dietary supplement specifically designed to support concentration, mental performance, and cognitive function. It contains a combination of essential vitamins, minerals, and high-quality botanicals, making it ideal for anyone looking to stay focused and alert throughout the day.

What’s Inside:

  • Vitamins B6 and B12 – Help reduce tiredness and fatigue, supporting mental alertness.
  • Pantothenic Acid (Vitamin B5) – Contributes to energy-yielding metabolism and mental performance.
  • Citicoline (Cognizin®) – Supports cognitive function and focus, also known as CDP-choline.
  • Zinc – Supports normal cognitive function, memory, concentration, and problem-solving ability.
  • Botanical Ingredients – Includes green tea, ginkgo leaf, guarana, rhodiola root, and bacopa herb extracts, which help improve focus and energy.

Product Benefits:

  • Boosts concentration and focus, perfect for studying, working, or tasks requiring mental effort.
  • Helps reduce mental fatigue and tiredness, keeping you alert throughout the day.
  • Contains natural botanicals like guarana (providing caffeine) and ginkgo, which support cognitive function and memory.

How to Use:

Take 4 capsules daily, preferably with food. Do not consume on an empty stomach.

Important Information:

  • Contains 50 mg of caffeine per daily dose.
  • Not recommended for pregnant or lactating women, or children under 18.
  • Do not exceed 800 mg of (-)-epigallocatechin-3-gallate per day. Avoid using other green tea products on the same day.
  • Store in a cool, dry place and keep out of reach of children.
