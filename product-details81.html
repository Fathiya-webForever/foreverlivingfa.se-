Product Description

Balancing Toner is designed to restore your skin’s natural pH balance after cleansing, ensuring that your skin is prepped for optimal absorption of subsequent skincare products. It gently removes any residual dirt and oils while soothing and freshening the skin.

Who is it for?

This toner is suitable for everyone who wants to maintain a healthy skin routine. Its mild yet effective formula works for all skin types, ensuring your skin is prepped and ready for creams, serums, or lotions.

Retain the Skin's Natural Balance

Tap water, often basic in nature, can disturb the skin’s natural pH after cleansing. Balancing Toner restores the skin’s natural, slightly acidic state, and helps close the pores, preventing dirt and grease from entering. It also removes any residues left behind by your cleanser, ensuring a deep cleanse.

Effective Ingredients

Balancing Toner contains nourishing and moisturizing ingredients to soothe and restore your skin:

  • Aloe Vera – Softens and smooths the skin.
  • Sorbitol – A humectant that helps retain moisture.
  • White Tea – Firms and strengthens the skin.
  • Cucumber – Hydrates and soothes.
  • Allantoin – Soothes and protects the skin.
  • Sodium Lactate & Sodium Hyaluronate – Moisturizing agents that enhance hydration.
  • Beta-Glucan & Red Seaweed – Nourish the skin and provide additional benefits.

Take Care of Your Skin

Balancing Toner is alcohol and perfume-free, making it ideal for all skin types. It restores moisture, balances the skin's pH, and provides a soothing, calming effect. Your skin will feel fresh, nourished, and ready for the next step in your skincare routine.

Dermatologically Tested

Balancing Toner received an EXCELLENT rating in dermatological tests performed by Dermatest®.

Usage Instructions

Apply a generous amount of Balancing Toner to a cotton pad and gently swipe it over the face and neck after cleansing. Use morning and evening for optimal results. Follow up with serum and moisturizing lotions for the best skincare benefits.
