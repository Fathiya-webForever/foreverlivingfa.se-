Product Description

Forever Fiber™ is a dietary supplement that helps you get the fibre you need without adding unnecessary calories. This water-soluble fibre powder is easy to mix into any beverage and helps promote digestive health and weight management.

What’s Inside:

  • Fibre – Dietary fibre from plant-based carbohydrates that doesn't break down in the digestive system.

Product Benefits:

  • Supports digestive health by improving stomach function.
  • Helps to manage weight by increasing fullness and reducing snacking.
  • Neutral taste that is easy to mix into beverages or sprinkle over food.
  • A convenient way to boost fibre intake without adding extra calories.

How to Use:

Take 1 portion pack (6.1 g) daily. Pour into a glass and mix with water or another beverage. Stir thoroughly until fully dissolved.

Important Information:

  • Do not exceed the recommended daily dose.
  • Keep in a cool, dry space and out of reach of children.
  • This product is a dietary supplement and not a substitute for a varied diet and healthy lifestyle.
