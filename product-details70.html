Product Description

Experience nature’s best with Aloe Body Lotion™. This fast-absorbing, non-greasy lotion blends Aloe vera, plant-based oils, and botanical extracts to deliver deep hydration and a silky-smooth finish.

Who is it for?

Ideal for anyone looking for soft, nourished skin. Aloe Body Lotion is perfect for daily use and absorbs quickly without leaving a greasy residue.

Key Benefits

  • Deeply rehydrates and conditions the skin.
  • Provides a silky smooth, non-greasy finish.
  • Contains argan oil and macadamia oil for enhanced nourishment.
  • Fast-absorbing formula with a light floral fragrance.
  • Perfect for all skin types.

How It Works

Aloe Body Lotion harnesses the power of natural ingredients to deeply condition and rehydrate your skin:

  • Aloe Vera – Provides intense hydration and skin-conditioning benefits.
  • Sodium Hyaluronate – Forms a protective barrier on the skin to retain moisture.
  • Jojoba Esters – Enhances skin firmness and elasticity.
  • Arginine – A naturally occurring amino acid that rehydrates and softens the skin.

Powerful Plant-Based Oils

  • Argan Oil – Enhances elasticity and contains vitamin E for a natural glow.
  • Macadamia Oil – Rich in palmitoleic acid to help maintain skin moisture levels.
  • Rosehip Oil & Jojoba Oil – Work together to nourish, condition, and soften the skin.

Usage Instructions

Apply Aloe Body Lotion generously to clean skin, massaging gently until fully absorbed. Use daily for best results.

Dermatologically Tested

Aloe Body Lotion™ was awarded the EXCELLENT rating in dermatological tests conducted by Dermatest®.

Important Information

For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. If irritation occurs, discontinue use. Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool, dry place.
